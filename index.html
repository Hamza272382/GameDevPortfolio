Galaxy Shooter

Galaxy Shooter is my first game, developed for both PC and mobile platforms. It's a fast-paced 2D space shooter where players control a spaceship to battle waves of enemy ships, dodge incoming attacks, and collect power-ups. Designed with simple controls and engaging gameplay. Creating Galaxy Shooter helped me learn key aspects of game development, including player movement,player health system, spawn manager, animations, shooting mechanics, enemy AI, UI design, main manu, game music implementation, game score, unity scene managemnt system and cross-platform implementation.
